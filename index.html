Our Philosophy

A different kind
of support.

Most people don't need a clinical therapist. They need a warm, wise human who actually shows up — not across a desk, but alongside them at routine events, daily activities, and fun outings.

We're bringing back what used to be natural: a trusted confidant, a friend who knows how to listen, a coach who walks through life with you — not a 50-minute office hour with a clipboard.

Alt Therapy is real-life coaching and companionship for modern humans navigating loneliness, love, and everything in between. We meet you where you are. Literally.

How It Works

Simple. Human. Yours.

No waiting rooms. No intake forms that take 45 minutes. Just tell us where you are and we design the support around your actual life.

01

Tell us what's going on

Use our intake tool above — select what you're navigating or describe it in your own words. Our AI reads between the lines and identifies exactly the right type of support for you.

02

We design your support

You receive a personalized care proposal — which type of session, which coach, what format (walks, coffee, home visits, activity sessions) — all shaped around how you actually live.

03

We show up in your world

Your coach meets you where you are. Grocery run feeling overwhelming? We come. Pre-event nerves? We're there. Couple in friction? We visit your home. Life-size support.
